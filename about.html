About Us

Restaurant Roulette is a food site for those who love to dine out but can't always decide on where. Wheter going out with friends who can't all agree on a new place to eat, simply tired of choosing the same places, or just can't seem to make up your mind --- Restaurant Roulette is here to help.

If you need a restaurant suggestion --- we keep it simple. Just type in your zip code, select how far you're willing to travel, type in a meal keyword (like tacos or Italian), and we'll do the rest.

Restaurant Roulette will search its database of thousands of restaurants and make a suggestion based on food reviews and ratings from people like you who love eating out. If our first selection is not what you have an appetite for, just scroll through our top picks. We're sure you'll find something that is just what you're looking for.

Give us a try and we'll give you a great place to eat.
